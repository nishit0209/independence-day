About Independence Day

Independence Day in India is celebrated on 15th August every year to honor the nation's freedom from British rule in 1947. The day is marked by the Prime Minister’s speech from the Red Fort, flag hoisting ceremonies, cultural programs, and parades. Citizens across the country celebrate with patriotism, unity, and pride.
